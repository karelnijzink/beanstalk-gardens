Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-04

This policy explains what personal information Beanstalk ([Legal business name — TBD], "we", "us") collects, why we collect it, and the choices you have. We follow Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and British Columbia's Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA).

1. What we collect

When you fill out our form, we collect only what we need to see if we can help you and to book your consult:

  • Your first name and email address;
  • Your phone number — only if you choose to give it, for a text reminder;
  • The first three characters of your postal code (your "FSA", e.g. V7E) — we use this to check our service area without needing your street address;
  • Your form answers about your space: municipality, whether you own or rent, yard type, sun, timeline, budget comfort band, your kids' age ranges (optional), and anything you tell us in the free-text box;
  • Your consent choice and the date/time you submitted the form;
  • Basic marketing attribution (which ad or link brought you here — UTM tags and, where applicable, Meta click identifiers).

We deliberately do not collect your street address, household income, or your children's names or birthdates at this stage. If you book a consult, our scheduling tool (Cal.com) will ask for your full address then, because we need it to visit.

2. Why we use it

  • To check whether your property is in our service area (Richmond, Delta, Surrey);
  • To send you your garden information and, if you qualify, a link to book a free consult;
  • To send booking confirmations and reminders about a consult you requested;
  • To send occasional Beanstalk tips and offers — only if you checked the marketing consent box (see "Your consent & email");
  • To measure which ads and content are working, so we don't waste money.

3. Your consent & email (CASL)

Our form's marketing checkbox is not pre-ticked. If you tick it, you give us express consent under Canada's Anti-Spam Legislation (CASL) to send marketing emails; we log your choice, the date/time, and the source. Emails about your own request — like your booking link and reminders — are sent because you asked for them.

Every marketing email includes who we are, our mailing address, and a one-click unsubscribe. You can unsubscribe at any time, and we honour it right away.

4. Cookies, Meta Pixel & Conversions API

We use the Meta (Facebook/Instagram) Pixel and Meta Conversions API to understand how our ads perform. These may set cookies (such as _fbp and _fbc) and share limited event information — for example that a page was viewed or a form was submitted — with Meta so we can measure and improve our ads. This data is handled under Meta's own terms and privacy policy.

You can limit this through your browser cookie settings and your Meta ad preferences. Blocking cookies will not stop you from using the form.

5. Where your information is stored & who can see it

Your information is stored in our customer records (Airtable) and used within our booking and email tools (for example Cal.com, Tally, our email sender, and our automation service n8n). These providers may store data outside British Columbia, including in the United States, and are subject to the laws of the countries where they operate. We share your information only with these service providers and only to run the service you asked for. We never sell your information.

6. How long we keep it

We keep your information for as long as needed to serve you and to keep proper business records. If you ask us to delete it, we will delete or anonymize your record within 30 days, except where we are required by law to keep certain records.

7. Your rights

You can ask us to see the information we hold about you, correct it, or delete it. To make a request, email hello@beanstalkgardens.ca. We may need to verify your identity first. We aim to respond within 30 days.

8. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information and limit access to the people and tools that need it. No system is perfectly secure, but we work to keep your data safe.
