Good questions

The things parents ask

Straight answers. The Garden Fit is free, and it comes with a real quote for your yard.

What does it cost?

Most family gardens land between $X,XXX and $X,XXX, depending on how many beds, irrigation, and the size of your space. Bigger yard, bigger number, and we will always be straight with you. Book my free consult.

I kill every plant I touch. Is this for me?

Especially for you. Most gardens fail because of the soil, not the person, and living soil is exactly what we build. About fifteen minutes a week, plus a care sheet and someone to text.

Can't I just build this myself?

You can, and some people love that. What you are paying us for is the design, the living soil that actually grows food, and a weekend of your life back. DIY gets you a box of dirt. We get you a garden that works.

We rent. Can we still do this?

Yes. We build planters and container gardens that move with you when you go, and we can give you a simple landlord letter if you want beds in the ground.

Is it too late to plant this year?

Almost never. Fall is BC's secret planting season, and a fall build sets you up for the best spring garden. If we are full for your window, we will save you a spot on the waitlist.
